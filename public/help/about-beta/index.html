What’s new in Streetmix beta?

New graphics

Say goodbye to the old silhouettes and say hello to a new visual style. City planning should be fun and friendly, and so can you!

New segments

Want a light rail? A bike rack? We have it all. We’ve also thrown in a trolley, bus shelters, different turn lanes, lane dividers, street furniture, wayfinding signs, and much, much more.

Save and remix streets

Have you made the world’s best street? Not to worry, Streetmix now saves all streets automatically as you change them. If you’re signed in with Twitter, we’ll remember it was yours, too. And if you see someone else’s street, you can take it and remix it with a single click.

Share with others

Tell all your friends about the new bike lane you put on your neighborhood street, or share your designs with your city officials. Send a link around, announce on via Twitter or Facebook, or export an image to use any way you like.

Learn more about urban design

Why care for bike lanes? What are sharrows? Where did parklets come from and what do they do? Find out more about all the things that can make your street a great street.

And more…

Wider streets (up to 400 feet), support for left-hand driving and metric system, and Streetmix now works on iPhone and iPad, too!

Go and build your street in Streetmix!
